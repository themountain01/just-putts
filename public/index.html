Chipping Lessons

Chipping is a huge aspect of the golfing game. Chipping is a way for you to reach the green and get as close to the pin as possible which would help to give yourself the best chance to putt in one. This is found to help lower your scores, thus improving your handicap.

Lessons On Putting

For our putting lessons we offer a variety of putting lessons as a way of focusing on the smaller key aspects of the putts that you make. These include: Short Putts, Long Putts, Lag Putts, Putting Technique, Learning how to read greens and optimising your ball roll.

Bunker Lessons

Bunker Shots are one of the most frustrating aspects in golf, therefore being able to get out first try is key. This bunker lesson helps to create a positive mindset once inside and is the ticket to getting you out in your first attempt as studies have shown your mindset is key in bunkers.

Around The Green

In and around the green requires techniques which can be taught by our specilist instructor, we provide different insights into approaching and executing the correct type of shot. These include: all types of spin shots and the different ways of getting as close to the pin as possible.
